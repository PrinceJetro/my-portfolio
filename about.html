My Journey in Tech & Education

From first lines of code to building platforms that transform learning

How It All Began

My coding journey started at 17 when I discovered I could make computers do amazing things with just text. What began as curiosity quickly became passion when I built my first project — JETRO'S GOLDEN CARS STORE, a supercar showcase website that displayed luxury cars for sale. That experience lit a fire in me to keep building and solving real-world problems through code.

Later, as a student at Yaba College of Technology, I saw firsthand how many students struggled with preparing for exams like JAMB, WAEC, and NECO. Despite having the drive to succeed, access to quality resources was limited. This inspired me to create Tutorial Haven — an EdTech platform designed to give students affordable, personalized, and AI-powered support right at their fingertips.

My first Deployed Project in 2022

The Vision Behind Tutorial Haven

Tutorial Haven was born out of a pressing need — to bridge the gap between ambition and access for students preparing for O-level exams like JAMB, WAEC, and NECO. I envisioned a platform where education meets innovation; where students can practice, learn, and grow with the help of intelligent tools and dedicated support.

As both founder and developer, I've poured myself into building every part of this platform — from the AI-powered tutoring system to the JAMB simulation engine. My goal has always been to simplify exam prep through technology, making it affordable, personalized, and truly effective for every Nigerian student.

"Students thrive when learning is made accessible, interactive, and aligned with the exams they face." - My Educational Philosophy

Technical Growth & Leadership

Building Tutorial Haven has been my greatest technical challenge and growth opportunity. Starting with basic web technologies, I've expanded my expertise to include:

  • Full-stack Python development (Django)
  • Educational technology architecture
  • AI integration for personalized learning
  • Scalable cloud infrastructure
  • Team leadership and technical mentorship

Leading a development team while growing the business has taught me the importance of clear communication, agile processes, and user-focused design. I've learned that great technology serves people first - whether it's our students or our development team.

My Mission

To empower Nigerian students with the tools, support, and confidence they need to excel in JAMB, WAEC, NECO, and beyond. Through Tutorial Haven, I aim to bridge the gap between quality education and accessibility—leveraging technology to deliver simplified learning, real-time practice, and personalized guidance to every student, regardless of their background.
