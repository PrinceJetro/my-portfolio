Website Cookies Explained: Should You Accept Them?

July 6, 2025 6 min read #Privacy #Cookies #WebSecurity #GDPR

Cookies are like tiny memory cards that websites leave in your browser to remember you. Some are helpful (like keeping you logged in), others track your activity. Here's what you need to know:

1. What Cookies Actually Do

  • Save shopping cart items
  • Store site preferences (dark mode/language)

How Cookies Work

  1. You visit example.com
  2. Website saves cookie in your browser
  3. On return, your browser shows the cookie
  4. Site "recognizes" you
// Example cookie content
{
  "userId": "123abc",
  "lastVisit": "2025-07-11",
  "preferences": {"darkMode": true}
}

Type Duration Purpose Risk
Session Until you close browser Keep you logged in Low

Third-party Varies Advertising/tracking High

Websites must:

üí° Smart Cookie Practices
