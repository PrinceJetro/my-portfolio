3 Months Into Uni & I'm Already Building the Faculty's Future Website — Here's Why 'Audacity' Changed Everything

March 20, 2026 5 min read #Unilag #Audacity #StudentEntrepreneur #BuildingInPublic #TechOnCampus

Last week, I attended an orientation.

I left with one word burned into my mindset:

AUDACITY

Not just intelligence.

Not just skill.

Not just hustle.

Audacity — the boldness to ask for what you haven't earned, to step where you haven't been invited, to build before you've been approved.

The Story That Lit the Fuse

A speaker shared her journey:

As a freshman, she wanted to host events but had no platform. No experience. No connections.

So one day, she walked up to the Faculty President and said:

"I want to host your next event."

No pitch deck. No portfolio. Just an ask. Pure audacity.

She got a yes.

That one yes led to 50+ events hosted.

That one moment of courage rewrote her entire university story.

I Decided to Follow That Blueprint

I looked at my own Faculty Student Association.

Strong on the ground — but no digital presence. No website. No centralized platform for students.

So I did the same.

1

Built a prototype in silence

No one asked me to

2

Drafted a clear, compelling proposal

Not just an idea, a vision

3

Reached out to the Faculty President

Directly, boldly, without waiting for an "opening"

And within hours — I got a response.

A YES.

Not just permission — but a mandate:

Build the future of the association online.

Why This Matters More Than Another Project

This isn't about adding a line to my portfolio.

✅ Proving that audacity works — You don't need to be "ready," you need to be bold
✅ Showing that problems are everywhere — The Faculty didn't know they needed this until someone pointed it out
✅ Demonstrating that you don't need permission to start — I built before I asked
✅ Creating opportunity through visibility — Now the entire faculty knows what I can do

The Real Takeaway — For Every Student, Builder, and Dreamer

🎨
Talent

is common.

📚
Skill

is learnable.

🧠
Wisdom

is valuable.

⚡
But audacity

is rare.

It's the difference between:

  • Dreaming of hosting events and asking to host one
  • Seeing a problem and volunteering to solve it
  • Waiting for an opportunity and creating your own

My Challenge to You This Week

Find one thing in your department, club, or community that's missing.

Build a solution — even if it's rough.

Then, ask for the opportunity to implement it.

Don't wait for an invitation.

Invite yourself.

Because sometimes, all it takes is one moment of boldness to change everything that comes after.

💡 The Core Truth

Audacity isn't recklessness — it's belief in action.

And I'm just getting started.
