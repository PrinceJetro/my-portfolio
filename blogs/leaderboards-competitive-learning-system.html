Feature Spotlight: The Leaderboards Powering Competitive Learning

March 20, 2026 7 min read #EdTech #Gamification #CompetitiveLearning #TutorialHaven #FeatureSpotlight

What It Is

A multi-layered leaderboard system designed to transform individual study into social, gamified competition. It ranks students across different dimensions to motivate consistent engagement and foster learning communities.

📊 The Three-Tiered Ranking Structure

📚 Subject-Based Leaderboards

Purpose: Encourage mastery in specific courses

Ranking Logic: Weighted score = Accuracy% × Consistency Bonus
  • Top 15 performers per subject
  • Dedicated subject leaderboard pages
🌍 Global Leaderboard

Purpose: Track overall platform standing

Ranking Logic: Total XP earned across all activities
  • Paginated results for browsing through thousands of learners
  • Global rankings page and mobile API endpoints
👥 Friends Leaderboard

Purpose: Foster friendly competition within social circles

Ranking Logic: XP comparison among connected users
  • Real-time updates as friends progress
  • Friends leaderboard views and social features

⚙️ Technical Implementation

API Endpoints

leaderboard(request) – Renders subject-based leaderboard pages with weighted scoring
leaderboards_global_paginated(request) – API endpoint for global XP rankings
leaderboards_friends(request) – API endpoint for friends-only XP comparisons
🎁 Referral Integration – Bonus XP (5 points) for successful friend invites

💭 Design Philosophy

Leaderboards serve multiple psychological needs:

🏃

Competitive Drive

Global rankings for broad comparison

🤝

Social Connection

Friends leaderboard for meaningful peer competition

🎯

Skill Development

Subject-specific rankings to encourage focused mastery

📈

Progress Visibility

Clear metrics show tangible growth over time

⚖️ Balancing Competition with Community

The system intentionally avoids creating purely extrinsic motivation by:

  • ✅ Celebrating multiple types of achievement (accuracy, consistency, persistence)
  • ✅ Balancing competition with community through friends-only views
  • ✅ Integrating with the broader XP and leveling system for holistic progression

"By making progress visible and social, the leaderboard system transforms solitary studying into a shared journey. Students don't just compete—they inspire each other, form study groups around subject rankings, and celebrate collective growth."

💡 Weighted Score Formula

Subject Score = (Accuracy% × 100) + (Consistency Bonus × 10)

Where Consistency Bonus rewards multiple attempts over time

💡 The Core Insight

The most effective leaderboards don't just rank—they reveal pathways to improvement. By showing students exactly where they stand and who's ahead, we transform abstract competition into concrete, achievable goals.
