From Platform to Pivot: Why We Removed Institution & Tutor Signups

March 17, 2026 7 min read #ProductPivot #EdTech #StartupJourney #PlatformStrategy #FeatureRemoval

🚀 Feature Spotlight: What changed… and why

In product development, sometimes the hardest decisions are removing features you've poured your heart into. Here's the story behind why we cut institution and tutor registration from Tutorial Haven—and what came next.

"The feature you're most proud of might be the one holding you back"

The Cold Reality: The "We'll Get Back to You" Wall

For months, I pursued the original vision relentlessly. I reached out to 50+ schools through every channel possible:

50+ Schools contacted
📍 Physical visits
📧 Email campaigns
📱 WhatsApp follow-ups
📞 Cold calls

I even visited my alma mater, demoed the platform to ICT staff. They loved it, provided valuable feedback. Energy was positive...

⏳ ...until the inevitable

"We'll get back to you"

The pattern was consistent: initial interest, acknowledgment of benefits, then radio silence. Schools already had platforms, legacy systems, or decision paralysis.

The Pivot Moment

The Realization Hit Hard:

If I couldn't get institutions onboard, the entire multi-tier architecture was a house of cards.

Decision Made: Remove institution and tutor registration flows entirely. Focus 100% on what I could control: direct student acquisition.

Why This Was Critical:

💰 Resource Allocation

Instead of chasing institutional approvals, I could focus on building features students actually wanted.

⚡ Velocity

Removing complex approval workflows sped up development significantly.

🎯 Ownership

I could validate the product without gatekeepers.

✨ The Unexpected Silver Lining: Custom Solutions

Here's the fascinating twist: While schools wouldn't adopt our platform, they had real needs we could solve differently.

I shifted from "use our platform" to "let me build what you need":

  • Pitched custom CBT (Computer-Based Test) platforms
  • Offered school portal development
  • Built complete website solutions
✅ SUCCESS STORY

POTENIA HEIGHTS COLLEGE

  • Built their entire website
  • Developed their custom CBT platform
  • Manage their school portal
  • Created a solution perfectly tailored to their workflow

🎯 Key Takeaways for Founders & Product Builders

👂 Listen to What Users Won't Do

Lack of adoption is data too. Sometimes the market is telling you to simplify.

🔄 Pivot ≠ Failure

Removing features can be strategic acceleration. Our student-focused growth exploded post-pivot.

🔍 Find the Hidden Need

Schools didn't want another platform. They did want custom solutions to specific problems.

💪 Your Skills Are Transferable

Platform building taught me what schools actually needed. I could monetize those insights differently.

📊 Current Status:

  • Tutorial Haven continues thriving as a student-focused platform
  • Less wasted energy chasing wrong-fit clients

🗣️ Question for You

Have you ever removed a core feature that wasn't working? How did it transform your business?

💡 The Hard Truth

Sometimes the most valuable product decision isn't what you add—it's what you have the courage to remove. The features you kill can teach you more than the ones you ship.
