Phone Proximity Sensors: Why Your Screen Turns Off During Calls

July 9, 2025 5 min read #TechExplained #Smartphones #Sensors

That magical moment when your phone "knows" it's at your ear isn't psychic ability - it's a clever little sensor working behind the scenes. Here's how this invisible tech protects your face and saves battery.

1. The Proximity Sensor Demystified

üìç Location

üîß Components

2. How It Works Step-by-Step

  1. IR Beam Fires: The LED emits invisible infrared light
  2. Object Detection: When your head approaches:
    • Light reflects off your ear/face
    • Detector notices the reflected light
  3. Signal Sent: Sensor tells processor: "Something's close!"
  4. Screen Off: Display powers down within milliseconds
