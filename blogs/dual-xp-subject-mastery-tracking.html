Leveling Up in Every Subject: How We Built Targeted Mastery Through Dual-XP Tracking

March 19, 2026 8 min read #EdTech #Gamification #PersonalizedLearning #MasteryBasedLearning #CompetitiveLearning

Master Every Subject, Not Just the Game

From general engagement to targeted academic growth

Traditional gamification often rewards general activity. At Tutorial Haven, we wanted to drive specialized mastery. So we built a dual-XP system that lets students level up not just overall, but in every single subject—turning broad engagement into targeted academic growth.

📊 How It Works: Two-Track Progression

Overall Progression
100% General XP
Subject-Specific XP
100% to Subject
50% to Overall

Each student has separate XP tracking for Biology, Maths, Chemistry, etc.

Quiz completions award:

  • ✅ Full XP to the subject-specific tracker
  • ✅ Half XP to overall progression

Result: Students advance in both general levels and subject mastery simultaneously

Independent Subject Levels

Every subject has its own level progression (Level 1–8). Higher subject levels unlock more challenging, discipline-specific questions.
