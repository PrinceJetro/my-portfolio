Tutorial Haven

Bridging education gaps in Nigeria through interactive learning

Launched: 2025 Active Visit Platform

Project Overview

Tutorial Haven was born out of a desire to make exam preparation more accessible, engaging, and effective for students across Nigeria. Frustrated by the lack of centralized, high-quality resources for JAMB, WAEC, and NECO, I created a platform that blends modern technology with personalized learning support.

As both founder and lead developer, I designed and built the platform from the ground up. Today, Tutorial Haven serves over 250 students across 10+ Nigerian states, helping them prepare confidently for their O-level exams through interactive tools and AI-powered guidance.

Key Features

  • AI-powered tutoring & concept explanations
  • Timed CBT simulations based on JAMB format
  • Subject-based past question practice (Theory + Objective)
  • Personalized progress tracking and feedback
  • Access to comprehensive, syllabus-based notes

Frontend

  • React
  • Redux for state management (Pending)
  • React Router for navigation

Backend

  • Django
  • Postgress For Database
  • Supabase For Storage
  • Vercel For Deployment

AI Components

  • Python with API
  • Custom code analysis models
  • Natural language processing
  • Recommendation algorithms

As Lead Developer

  • Designed and developed the full-stack infrastructure of the platform
  • Integrated AI-powered tools for answering students' questions and recommending study plans
  • Created modules for CBT simulations, note delivery, and live class integration
  • Led the development of a scalable backend to support thousands of student users

As CEO

  • Defined the vision of democratizing access to O-level exam prep resources
  • Established collaborations with schools and education influencers across Nigeria
  • Bootstrapped the business with personal funds and support from early users
  • Recruited and coordinated a small team of tutors and volunteer content creators
